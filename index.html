Snow fall story


Snow's cold, moist surface influences how much heat and moisture circulate between the ground and the atmosphere. Snow helps insulate the ground below, holding in heat and preventing moisture from evaporating into the atmosphere.One of the best places to see snowfall in Himachal Pradesh, Shimla, also referred to as the summer capital, offers an altogether a different experience during winters. Wait till the month of December, and the view of its snow-clad landscapes may just blow your mind! Drawing hordes of tourists, especially honeymooners and families, this hill station has got every kind of traveler, including nature lovers, adventure-junkies, and history buffs covered. It is one of the best options for Himachal snowfall places.



Snow Lake




Grab an ultimate opportunity to witness snow rains and snowballs on an excursion to Spiti Valley. Albeit, the best time to explore through the breathtaking environs of this place is the summer season; the winters of Spiti are meant for adventure-junkies like you! Crammed with monasteries and picturesque lakes, the view of this region is completely beguiling during winters. While, indulging in experiences like riding on the Kunzum range, stopping by Chandratal Lake, and gorging on momos and Thukpa at Tibetan restaurants make it even more alluring.
